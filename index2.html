Services

Whether you're looking to update just one room or a whole house, our talented design team are ready to help. With over seventeen years of experience, we have completed projects all over the world. We provide a range of bespoke services which are both approachable and flexible - we can help with anything from pulling together a flat, pied-a-terre, country house or a property abroad. Whether you just wish to add a few statement pieces of furniture to complete a room, or you’re starting from scratch with a whole house – we would love to help. We don’t just focus on furniture or accessories; we can also help with anything from flooring, to paint colours, to curtains. At OKA, we believe in incorporating your existing furniture, so we will always make space for your most loved pieces. We work with clients all over the world, in person, from plans or blue prints. We arrange shipping to far-flung places and are happy to oversee installation if required.

Designers

CEO & Founder

John has overseen the design of Chateau Gütsch in Lucerne, Switzerland, and Chef Rick Bayless’s flagship Red O restaurant; he is currently overseeing hotel designs in Santa Barbara, Istanbul, and Miami Beach, along with the Four Seasons Residences in Los Angeles.

Jane Dawson
Designer

Jane’s crocheted portraiture is a fascinating combination of traditional technique with contemporary subject matter. She has made large-scale cityscapes as well as larger than life portraits, ranging from friends, to commission subjects.

Mike Ross
Architect

One of the leading architects of his generation and a global cultural ambassador for the UK, London-based, Ghanaian-British architect Mike Ross believes in the power of architecture to achieve positive social change.
